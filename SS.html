Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is known as Earth’s sister planet because of its similar size. It has a very thick atmosphere made mostly of carbon dioxide, which makes it the hottest planet in the solar system.

Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet that supports life. About 71% of Earth’s surface is covered with water, and it has an atmosphere rich in oxygen.

Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is called the Red Planet because of its reddish surface. Scientists believe Mars may have once had water, and many space missions are exploring it for signs of life.
